About Global Daily News

Global Daily News is a fictional news website created for demonstration purposes. Our goal is to deliver high-quality news content across multiple categories including World, Business, and Technology.

We aim to provide readers with clear, informative, and engaging articles. All content is written by experienced journalists and contributors who are passionate about keeping you informed on current events.

This website is designed as a fully front-end, static website skeleton suitable for showcasing a professional news portal. It is ideal for learning, portfolio purposes, or as a base for further development.
